Ideal Medical Laboratory Survey

Introduction

Greetings,

We are pleased to invite you to participate in this scientific survey, which is part of a graduation project in Medical Equipment Engineering at Al-Bayan University for Science and Technology titled "The Ideal Medical Laboratory." This survey aims to collect accurate and comprehensive data about the reality of medical laboratories from technical and administrative perspectives, focusing on maintenance, equipment failures, result accuracy, safety, and accreditation.

Your valuable participation in this survey will effectively contribute to developing an integrated vision for the ideal medical laboratory and will help identify the real challenges facing medical laboratories and ways to overcome them. Your answers will also contribute to providing practical and innovative solutions to improve the performance of medical laboratories and ensure the quality of their services.

We assure you that all data and information you provide will be treated with complete confidentiality and will only be used for scientific research purposes. Completing this survey takes approximately 10-15 minutes of your valuable time.

Thank you for your cooperation and valuable contribution to the success of this scientific project.
